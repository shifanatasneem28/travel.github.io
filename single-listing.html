Madras Pavilion

ITC Grand Chola, No., 63, Anna Salai, Guindy, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600032
  • Overview
  • Menu

  • Location on map

Madras Pavilion’s spread is sumptuously expansive and its service, genuine; but unlike sister restaurants at the Grand Chola, the food is neither best-in-class, nor particularly memorable. Idlis, eggs, bakes and amp; breads, soups and roasts are all above par The breakfast filter coffee ritual is truly theatrical: a veshti-clad bearer will enthusiastically pour filter coffee back and forth across a height of over a metre, while beaming at you with the practiced smile of a seasoned performer. Keep a camera handy Even though the Madras Pavilion and #39;s floor space matches upto ITC Grand Chola standards, during peak hours (like breakfast) things get a little too buzzy. Sit away from the island kitchen and towards the corners to escape the hubbub

“The best Burger in town”

Ensconced in a regal atrium amidst spectacular chandeliers, Madras Pavilion falls right in with the grand ITC design scheme; however in the light of nine other dining options at this massive hotel, some of which are trendsetting pioneers, it does not try hard enough to prevent being overshadowed. At some moment during a chaotic buffet meal, as you sidestep patrons and try to flag bearers amidst the din for an odd dish that is nearly forgotten, the grandeur around Madras Pavilion can seem exasperatingly transient.

Swathi Priya S

12 June 2020

“Quality ingredients”

I ate dinner 3 times at the hotel while staying there and I can tell you that the food is top class and the ingredients are fresh and got quality.Needless to say that the breakfast is lovely and plentiful, the dinner itself is also recommended.They cook to order and they buy the ingredients daily and the food is fresh (lamb,fish,duck etc)I ate the lamb chops and the duck (once with caramelized fruits and once with potatoes)the concept of the restaurant is that you pay as much as you want, and they say if you didn't like the food than you don't have to pay. obviously that was never the case because the food was great. I asked them to make papanasi for me because I never tried a Romanian traditional dessert . they made it fresh and at the end they told me "it's on the house"So like I reviewed the hotel and pointed that he is very welcoming, I can say the same thing about the restaurant.

Diviya S

12 August 2020
